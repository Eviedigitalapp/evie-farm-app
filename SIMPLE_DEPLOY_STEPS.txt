SUPER SIMPLE DEPLOYMENT STEPS
==============================

Your app is READY! Here's what to do:

STEP 1: Get the files
---------------------
You need to download this folder from your project:
   /workspaces/default/code/dist

OR download this file:
   /workspaces/default/code/evie-farm-deploy.tar.gz
   (Then extract/unzip it)

STEP 2: Go to Netlify
---------------------
Open this link in your web browser:
   https://app.netlify.com/drop

You should see a big box that says "Drag and drop your site folder here"

STEP 3: Upload
--------------
Drag the 'dist' folder (or extracted folder) into that box

STEP 4: Wait
------------
Wait 30 seconds. Netlify will give you a URL.

DONE!
=====
Your app is live at that URL!

DON'T FORGET: Deploy Supabase server from Figma Make settings!
